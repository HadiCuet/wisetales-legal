Frequently asked questions

About the app

What is Wise Tales?
Wise Tales is a bilingual folk-tale reader for children. Every tale is read sentence by sentence, showing the story's language and your chosen language side by side — so children pick up a second language in context while enjoying a story. It supports 13 languages — English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Bengali, Arabic, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, and Turkish — in any pair, and can read every tale aloud. It works fully offline.

Is the app free?
Yes, the app is free to download. A handful of tales are free to read in full, and every other tale's first page is free to preview. A single one-time "Unlock all tales" purchase opens the whole library — there's no subscription.

What ages is this for?
Primarily ages 4–10, but anyone learning a second language alongside a fluent speaker (a parent, grandparent, or sibling) will enjoy the side-by-side reader.

Purchases

How do I unlock all the tales?
Open Settings (the gear icon) → Wise Tales Premium → Unlock all tales, or tap any locked tale and turn to its second page. It's a one-time purchase through Apple — not a subscription.

How do I restore a previous purchase?
Open Settings → Restore purchases (also available on the unlock screen). Make sure you're signed into the App Store with the Apple ID you used to make the purchase.

Does the unlock support Family Sharing?
Yes. The one-time unlock is shareable with up to 5 additional family members in your Apple Family Sharing group — one purchase, six readers.

I bought the unlock but tales are still locked.
Tap Restore purchases first — that resolves almost every case. If it still shows locked, email us and we'll sort it out within 48 hours.

Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Wise Tales purchase, and request a refund. Apple typically responds within 48 hours.

Using the app

How do I change the reading languages?
Open Settings (the gear icon) → Languages. Pick a Reading language (the language of the story) and a Translation language (shown beneath each sentence). Tap the swap button to flip the two.

How does "read to me" work?
Open a tale and start narration — Wise Tales reads each sentence aloud and highlights the words as it goes. Under Settings → Read to me you can choose the storyteller voice, the reading speed, a sleep timer, and Echo (which reads each sentence again in your translation language). Narration keeps playing while the screen is locked.

Does it work offline?
Yes, completely. Every tale, every translation, and the narration all run on your device — no internet connection is needed.

Privacy and data

What data does the app collect?
Only basic, anonymized usage analytics — for example, which tales are opened and finished — never linked to your identity, and never the story text or your translations. There's no account or login. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Is this safe for children?
Yes. The app contains no ads, no third-party tracking, no in-app links to the web, and no user-generated content. The only outbound network calls are anonymous analytics pings to Firebase, used solely to decide which tales and features to improve.
